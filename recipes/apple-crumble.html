Apple Crumble

Make the best apple crumble in the world at home!

  • 125g Butter
  • 150g Flour
  • 50g Almonds
  • 2 Packets of vanilla sugar
  • 70g Sugar
  • 3 Big apples
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celcius.
  2. Melt the butter in a pot.
  3. Mix flour, sugar, vanilla sugar, almonds and the liquid butter in a bowl to make the crumble dough.
  4. Cut the apples into slices and spread them evenly on a baking tray.
  5. Cover the apples on the tray with the crumble dough.
  6. Bake it for 20 minutes.
